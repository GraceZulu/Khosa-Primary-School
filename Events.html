Sports Day

Our Sports Day is held on the third Thursday of the second school term.




Trips Overseas

Exposure to new cultures can significantly benefit a child's personal development. School travel exposes students experiences they wouldn't usually have access to. Students will gain broader perspectives and learn to be comfortable in unfamiliar situations. School travel can be more meaningful for students than holiday and leisure travel. Away from family and friends' students are compelled to step outside their comfort zone. As well as learn, students will meet new people, try new foods, travel foreign places, and so much more.



Awards Giving Ceremony

At the end of each school term, the school holds its termly awards giving ceremony to the award the most outstanding learners. During this ceremony, learners who were the best in various subjects are given personal shields, metals and koala bears.
